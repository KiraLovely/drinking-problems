What is this Website about?

This website is supposed to be an archive for, specifically, if you haven't noticed, Gamersupps. It's a fan-project, so nothing official. Anything ever made by Gamersupps, or anyone affiliated with them, will be archived here. Why? Because WE love Gamersupps, and preserving their history is important to us. (And because they aren't really doing anything themselves to preserve, well, nothing that's public). Many products have been lost to time, and are removed from the official website. That's why we've decided, it's time to archive! Because sometimes, you just want to know when and where!
