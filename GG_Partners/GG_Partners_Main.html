What is this?

This website is supposed to be an archive. Specifically, if you haven't noticed, for Gamersupps. It's a fan-project, so nothing official. We ARE NOT Gamersupps, as so, we do NOT show any leaked content. Anything ever made by Gamersupps, or anyone affiliated with them, will be archived here. Why? Because WE love Gamersupps, and preserving it is important. Many products go lost in time, or removed from the official website. That's why we decided, it's time to archive!
